Are you the next Robin Hood?

Enjoy a day at Kirchschlag learning about the ancient sport of archery. This experience is great for all levels of ability, so come and learn how to become a master of archery.

Our club started life at the beginning of 2008, since then we've been going from strength to strength. We are located in Kirchschlag, just a couple of minutes outside of Linz. We absolutely love everything about archery and we think we have the best location in the country for accessibility for the most people who would like to get into the sport. We would love to introduce you to archery whether it's through a Have a Go session, a work event or finding your feet with a club for the first time. We have both Target and Field archery and a regular clout shoot. We welcome archers with Recurve, Compound and Long Bows and all types of Field Bows. Cross Bows are not allowed at this club.
